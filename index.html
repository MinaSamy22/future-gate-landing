A Parent-Child Wallet Built on Trust

A Trusted Digital Wallet for Parents and Children: Parents Reward, Children Grow and Earn!

Why Gifted Wallet?

Customized Chores

Parents assign chores tailored to each child's needs, promoting growth and learning.

Instant Points Transfer

Once a chore is approved, points are transferred directly to the child.

Spend Notifications

Parents are aware of their children's expenses

Secure Spending

Children can safely spend earned points from trusted partners.

How it work?

As Parent

1
Download the app and sign up.
2
Add your children's details.
3
Set chores and rewards.
4
Evaluate and accept tasks upon completion.

As Child

1
Download the app and sign in.
2
Complete the chores.
3
Submit tasks for review.
4
Earn and redeem points for digital gifts.
